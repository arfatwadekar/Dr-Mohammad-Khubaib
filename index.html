About Us

At Dr. Mohammad Khubaib's Mumbra Health Care Centre, we are dedicated to providing accessible, high-quality homoeopathic healthcare for the Mumbra community. Our focus is on compassionate care, modern computerized consulting, physiotherapy, counseling, and building trusted patient relationships.

At Dr. Mohammad Khubaib's Mumbra Health Care Centre, our mission is to empower the Mumbra community with reliable, compassionate, and modern homoeopathic healthcare. We simplify complex medical processes, provide trusted guidance through counseling, and support families with care built on innovation and integrity. With facilities including physiotherapy, blood & urine testing, ECG, dietician services, and expertised cupping therapy (Hijama), we offer comprehensive health solutions under one roof.

Our vision is to be Mumbra's most trusted healthcare partner — known for exceptional service, patient-centered solutions, and empowering healthier lives across the community.

What started as a small local practice quickly grew into a respected community clinic — driven by dedication, patient trust, and a commitment to delivering advanced healthcare close to home.
